About Us

CraveBite's Background

In the vibrant landscape of culinary delights, CraveBite emerged as a beacon of excellence on March 26, 2022. Situated at the heart of Kompleks PKNS Shah Alam, 11 Persiaran Sultan, Seksyen 14, 40000 Shah Alam, Selangor, CraveBite beckons cookie enthusiasts into a realm where each bite is a journey of flavor and craftsmanship. The founders envisioned CraveBite not merely as a bakery but as a destination, where premium cookies become an art form. Operating within the dynamic food and baked goods industry, CraveBite distinguishes itself by focusing exclusively on premium cookies. In a market inundated with mass-produced options, CraveBite takes a deliberate step towards quality over quantity.

The company's main activity revolves around the production and sale of premium cookies, where each creation is a testament to the dedication to perfection. The heart and soul of CraveBite's offerings lie in its diverse range of premium cookies. From the timeless allure of classic chocolate chip to the adventure of exotic blends, CraveBite's creations are a symphony of flavors, textures, and aromas. Every cookie embodies a meticulous fusion of traditional baking techniques and the use of premium ingredients, ensuring that each bite is a moment to savor. It is a welcoming space, inviting cookie connoisseurs to explore and savor the delectable creations.

The business hours, extending from 10:00 AM to 10:00 PM every day of the week, underscore CraveBite's commitment to accessibility and convenience. As CraveBite continues to carve its niche in the industry, the company envisions a future marked by innovation and unwavering customer satisfaction. With each batch of cookies, CraveBite not only aims to tantalize taste buds but also to create lasting memories and moments of pure delight.
